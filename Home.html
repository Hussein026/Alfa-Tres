Lenovo Group Ltd. is a Chinese multinational technology company founded in 1984. It is a leading global manufacturer of personal computers, smartphones, tablets, servers, and other electronic devices. Acquiring IBM's PC business in 2005 catapulted Lenovo to the forefront of the industry, establishing it as the world's largest PC vendor.

HP (Hewlett-Packard) is a renowned American multinational technology company headquartered in Palo Alto, California.
Founded in 1939 by Bill Hewlett and David Packard, it has since become a leading provider of personal computing devices, printers, and related services globally.
HP's product range includes laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories, catering to both consumer and business markets.

The company produces a wide range of products, including desktop and laptop computers, tablets, monitors, projectors, and gaming devices.
Acer is known for its innovative designs, cutting-edge technology, and affordable pricing, making its products popular among consumers and businesses worldwide.
With a commitment to research and development.
